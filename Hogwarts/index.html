About Us

Hogwarts is a magical school found in the Harry Potter films. In the Harry Potter films, Hogwarts was founded in 1000 AD

Hogwarts Studio is a visiting place that is the same as Hogwarts School. Hogwarts Studio was only founded around 2020 and is located in the city of London, United Kingdom. When you visit Hogwarts Studio, you can feel something real about being a student at Hogwarts School. At Hogwarts Studio too, there are many places that are very similar to Hogwarts School. Then, during this visit you can get a discount on ticket prices on certain days. Apart from that, when you visit Hogwarts you will get a special small gift.

Map

You will get a free map service so you don't get lost and the map can also be a memory for you when visiting Hogwarts Studio

Tour Guide

When visiting Hogwarts Studio, you will get an experienced tour guide so you will really enjoy your visit to Hogwarts Studio.

Souvenir

You will get a special little gift related to Harry Potter. Then, Hogwarts Studio also provides a souvenir shop.

Our Packages

There are three types of packages to reserve when you want to visit Hogwarts Studio. Each package has different benefits.

Single Tour

You will get a private tour guide and special service for your visit to Hogwarts Studio

Couple Tour

You will get a private tour guide for your visit with your partner to Hogwarts Studio and you will get a discount because you are a couple

Group Tour

You will get a tour guide for your group and your expenses will be more economical

Transport

On tours visiting Hogwarts Studio, transportation is provided to move from one place to another because the distance is too far.

Food & Drink

You will get free food and drink when youvisiting Hogwarts Studio, so you don't worry to be hungry.

Free Costume

You will get a service to borrow a Harry Potter costume so you will feel like you are a real student at Hogwarts School

First Aid

You will get a first aid health service for safety so you don't need to worry when visiting Hogwarts Studio
